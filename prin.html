"A school is a building that has four walls with tomorrow inside. This is because the destiny of man is shaped in the class room of the school."

Welcome to Brahmi Janta Sr. Sec. School. Since its founding in 2016, BJS has been dedicated to providing the best elementary education to our students. As an educator I had an earnest desire to build up an institution where an education blended in all colors of human values could be given and today I feel proud to say that my lofty dream of a quality institution is taking shape.

We dedicate ourselves to make our students compassionate, creative, and filled with desire to meet new challenges and pursue creative paths to solutions. Our amazing teachers work hard to create an exceptional learning environment for our students. As a result, our school has scored 100% result in Board Examinations for class 10th and 12th from the past 5 years.

I believe my role as an educator is to guide and nurture the next generation to establish skills to achieve health, respect, prosperity and fulfillment. Our major focus is on leadership which begins in the classrooms of our youngest students and culminates in each grade when our oldest students take on the responsibility of being the school's younger student’s leaders. I am passionate about students being empowered to succeed. As Chairman, I use all tools available to inspire staff, students and community to work together to promote student achievement and well-being.

Throughout the year, exciting things are happening in our classrooms and on our campus! We encourage you to get involved by taking an active role in your child's education, which is the best gift you can give your child. It is my desire to keep the focus on increasing student achievement. All our students be successful and enjoy coming to school each day. I believe that learning should be fun. I believe that having quality, inspirational and passionate teachers is essential to a school's success.To borrow John Hattie's words; "teachers should build classroom climates where errors are welcome, student engagement is the norm, questioning is high and students gain reputations as effective learners."

I give all my blessings to the BJS, to the staff, students, parents and all those associated with this Institution. I pray that God, in His divine benevolence, continues to lead and guide this prestigious Institution for generations to come.



DR. SURENDER SINGH
(M.A., B.Ed, M.Ed, Ph.D)
CHAIRMAN
