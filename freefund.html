School Pledge

We the students of Brahmi Janta Sr. Sec. School swear that we
shall abide by all rules and regulations of the school. We
promise to keep our school and environment clean and green.
We will give respect to all our parents, teachers and elders.
We all love our motherland and feel proud of her rich culture and
great heritage. We shall always strike to be worthy of her and we
promise to be loyal to our country and countrymen.



Prayer of a Student

Give me an attentive mind, Lord, Keen and Alert
So, that I can concentrate and grasp my lessons.
Give me an egar mind to learn something new every day.
And my aim always be to pierce the evil of the visible
world to reach you.
